Bitcoin TX - Digital Assets Management

Decentralized Multisignature Storage Solution for Digital Assets Custody


  • Geographic Distribution: Assets are stored across multiple remote locations to enhance security and reduce the risk of centralized failure.
  • Secure Storage: All digital asset keys are safeguarded in both private and institutional safe deposit boxes, ensuring confidentiality and protection.
  • Fee Structure:
    • Withdrawals: A maximum fee of 1% is applied, with reductions available through a quantity discount formula.
    • Deposits: No fees are charged for deposits.
