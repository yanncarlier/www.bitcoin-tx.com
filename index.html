Bitcoin TX - Digital assets management

Decentralized offline storage solution for digital assets custody

Geographic distribution: Assets are stored across multiple remote locations to enhance security and reduce the risk of centralized failure.

Secure Storage: All digital asset keys are safeguarded offline in both private and institutional safe deposit boxes, ensuring protection, redundancy, integrity, and confidentiality.

Fee Structure: Withdrawals: A maximum fee of 1% is applied, with reductions available through a quantity discount formula. Deposits: No fees are charged for deposits.
